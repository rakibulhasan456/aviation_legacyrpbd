AVIATION RULES & REGULATIONS

  1. Failed to show flying activity.
  2. Failed to show ID card while on flight activity.
  3. If not wearing uniform while on duty.
  4. If not on the radio while on flight.
  5. Involved in criminal activities.
  6. Valid complaint from citizen/other department against pilot.
  7. Recruitment corruption activities.
  8. Giving aircraft keys to others except pilots/students.
  9. Disobeying Instructor pilot.
  10. Taking passenger flight without ticket.
  11. Not following taxiway, runway waypoint and helipad.
  12. Using aircraft smoke unnecessarily.
  13. Performing stunt without G-Suit.
  14. Flying damaged aircraft.
  15. Operating aircraft without parachute.
  16. Aircraft not being parked properly.
  17. Continuous bad flying record.
  18. Stealing others' aircraft/helicopter.
  19. If the private car is not parked in the parking area.

Punishment & Suspension

1st strike : $5,000 fine / License suspended for 7 days

2nd strike : $10,000 fine / License suspended for 7 days
